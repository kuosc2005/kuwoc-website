We welcome contributions from the community! Whether you're fixing a typo, improving documentation, or adding new features, your help is appreciated.

## How to Contribute

- **Report Issues:** Found a bug or have a suggestion? Open an issue on our repository.
- **Submit Pull Requests:** Have a fix or improvement? Submit a PR for review.
- **Improve Documentation:** Help others by clarifying or expanding our docs.
- **Share Feedback:** Let us know how we can improve the event experience.

:::note[Before Contributing]
Please review our organization's [Contributing Guidelines](https://github.com/kuosc2005) to understand our code of conduct, development workflow, and submission requirements.
:::

## Getting Started

Ready to contribute? Here's how:

1. **Fork the repository** on GitHub
2. **Clone your fork** to your local machine
3. **Create a new branch** for your changes
4. **Make your changes** and commit them
5. **Push to your fork** and submit a pull request

Need help? Check out our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/kuosc2005) or reach out to the team if you have questions.
