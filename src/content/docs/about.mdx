# About KU Winter of Code

KU Winter of Code (KUWoC) is an annual open-source contribution program organized by the  
**Kathmandu University Open Source Community (KUOSC)**, under the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, School of Engineering, Kathmandu University.

The event is designed to introduce students to real-world open-source development by connecting them with organizations, projects, mentors, and collaborative communities.
